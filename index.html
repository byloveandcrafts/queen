✦ Good to Know ✦

🌿 The ceremony & reception are held outdoors on a beach. The venue is on sand — please skip the stilettos or super high heels! Wedge heels, block heels, sandals, or dressy flats are your best friends for comfort and style on sandy ground.

🌞 Bring a mini fan — it's June in Cebu and it will be warm, especially during the outdoor ceremony. Your future self will thank you!

🧴 Pack sunscreen — the beachside ceremony starts at 4:00 PM and the sun can still be quite strong. A quick layer before you arrive goes a long way.
